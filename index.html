George Floyd Memorial Bust



This is a 3D bust of George Floyd which can be seen in Augmented Reality by opening this page on a supported iOS or Android device.

The bust was created by the father and son team of sculptors, Daniel and Rodman Edwards, and was originally posted on Sketchfab by Daniel Edwards to allow supporters of the Black Lives Matter protests to 3D print it at home. As not many of us do not have access to a 3D printer, I created this page to allow people to place the memorial in their homes through Augmented Reality.
